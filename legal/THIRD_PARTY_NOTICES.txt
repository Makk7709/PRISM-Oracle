LICENSE AUDIT NOTES (Internal self-declaration)
================================================================================

History of internal audits:

(1) Initial audit dated 2025-02-08 (covering ~372 packages at that date):
    At that date, no GPL, AGPL, or SSPL licensed dependencies were identified
    as direct dependencies. The original markdown-pdf dependency (which pulled
    PyMuPDF/AGPL) was replaced by a local MIT-licensed shim using reportlab
    (BSD). Two further GPL-only direct dependencies have since been removed
    as documented in requirements.txt:
      - "ansio removed - GPL v3, not used anywhere in codebase"
      - "html2text removed - GPL-3.0, replaced by markdownify (MIT)"

(2) Updated audit via pip-licenses 5.5.1 dated 2026-05-15
    (full report: docs/annexes-externes/AE-11_pip-licenses_2026-05-15.md
     synthesis: docs/annexes-externes/AE-11_pip-licenses_README.md):

    Confirmed:
      - 0 GPL-only packages (direct or transitive)
      - 0 AGPL packages
      - 0 SSPL packages

    Identified and disclosed (compatible with commercial proprietary use):
      - 5 LGPL packages used by dynamic Python import (LGPL-compatible):
          chardet (LGPLv2+), crontab (LGPLv2/v3), num2words (LGPL),
          paramiko (LGPL), pi_heif (LGPLv3)
      - 5 MPL 2.0 packages (file-level copyleft, unmodified):
          certifi, legacy-api-wrap, pathspec, pikepdf, tqdm (dual MIT/MPL)
      - 1 triple-licensed package usable under MPL 1.1 (no GPL contamination):
          pyphen (GPLv2+ OR LGPLv2+ OR MPL 1.1)
      - 1 isolable risk: espeakng-loader 0.2.4 dynamically loads
        eSpeak NG (GPL v3+). Used only for optional TTS (text-to-speech)
        functionality, NOT part of the 17 proprietary KOREV Evidence modules
        scoped for valuation. Can be removed without impacting valued scope.

This audit is an internal self-declaration and is NOT a third-party legal
opinion or certified license scan. For external valuation, due diligence,
or commercial transfer purposes, an independent third-party license scan
may be requested by Diag & Grow / commissaire aux apports.
